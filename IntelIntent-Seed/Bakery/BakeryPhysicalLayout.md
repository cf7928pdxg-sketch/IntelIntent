## Key Service Areas

### Service Bar
The service bar is the main customer-facing production area where bagels are displayed and orders are assembled. It has two sides:

1. **Customer Side (FOH-SRV-CUST)**
   - Display case for bagels
   - Menu board
   - Customer viewing area

2. **Employee Side (FOH-SRV-EMP)**
   - Assembly stations
   - Bagel slicers
   - Cream cheese dispensers
   - Sandwich preparation area

### Checkout Area (FOH-CHECKOUT)
- Cash registers
- Point of Sale system
- Refrigerated display for non-alcoholic beverages (Coke and Dr. Pepper)

### Coffee Bar (FOH-COFFEE)
- Coffee brewing equipment
- Self-service cream and sugar station
- Cup dispensers
- Condiment station

### Snapple Cooler (FOH-SNAP)
- Branded display cooler
- Primary storage for TKO products
- Secondary storage for other cold retail items

## Production Areas

### Preparation Area (BOH-PREP)
- Mixers for dough preparation
- Work tables for forming bagels
- Storage for ingredients in active use

### Oven Area (BOH-OVEN)
- Bagel ovens
- Loading and unloading stations
- Heat-resistant work surfaces

### Cooling Area (BOH-COOL)
- Racks for fresh-baked products
- Staging for transfer to FOH

## Storage Areas

### Ambient Storage (BOH-STORAGE-AMB)
- Shelving for dry ingredients
- Packaging materials storage
- Non-refrigerated supplies

### Refrigerated Storage (BOH-STORAGE-REF)
- Walk-in cooler
- Dough retarding
- Dairy product storage
- Prepared item storage

### Freezer Storage (BOH-STORAGE-FRZ)
- Frozen ingredient storage
- Long-term inventory storage
- Backup product storage

## Vendor Managed Areas

### Coke Refrigerator (FOH-BVNDR-COKE)
- Coke-branded refrigerated display
- Maintained by Coca-Cola vendor
- Located in customer-accessible retail area

### Dr. Pepper Refrigerator (FOH-BVNDR-DP)
- Dr. Pepper-branded refrigerated display
- Maintained by Dr. Pepper vendor
- Located in customer-accessible retail area

## Traffic Flow

### Customer Flow
- Entry → Menu viewing → Order placement → Coffee service → Checkout → Exit

### Employee Flow
- BOH preparation → Transfer to FOH → Customer service → Restock as needed

### Inventory Flow
- Delivery (rear entrance) → BOH storage → Preparation → Service → Sale
